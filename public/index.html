Street Patrolling

This is not a new concept. It is a concept whereby security officers are placed on the ground, patrolling a designated either on foot or by bicycle. So ensuring visibility in the area and stamping out crime.
This concept was taken over from the English, where the "Bobby"(police officer) patrols the streets, gathering intelligence and being the eyes and ears of the community.
